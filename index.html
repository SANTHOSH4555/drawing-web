About the Artist

I am a passionate visual artist dedicated to exploring the intersection of precise detail and emotive storytelling. Transitioning into the digital and creative fields, my work is driven by a deep fascination with light, form, and human expression.

Through "Santhosh Dream Drawing", I aim to bring imaginations to life on canvas and paper, crafting pieces that leave a lasting impression on viewers and clients alike.
