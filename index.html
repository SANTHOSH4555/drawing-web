About the Artist

Drawing, for me, is not just a skill — it is a way of expressing what words often cannot. Every portrait I create carries a story, an emotion, and a moment that deserves to be remembered. I focus on capturing not just the face, but the feeling behind it. My journey started with a simple interest in art, but over time it became a passion that I continue to explore and improve every day. Through my work, I aim to create art that feels personal, meaningful, and real.

Through "Santhosh Dream Drawing", I aim to bring imaginations to life on canvas and paper, crafting pieces that leave a lasting impression on viewers and clients alike.
